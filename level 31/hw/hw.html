"100 words" is a creative writing exercise and platform where writers craft stories, reflections, or journal entries in exactly 100 words, fostering focus, self-expression, and daily writing habits. Overview The concept of "100 words" is both a writing challenge and a platform. Websites like 100words.com and 100words.app encourage writers to create entries limited to 100 words, which helps sharpen writing skills, clarify thoughts, and maintain a daily writing habit. This concise format forces writers to focus on essential ideas, turning casual thoughts into structured, meaningful content.
