Christopher Larkin

Christopher James Larkin[1] was born in Adelaide, South Australia.[citation needed] Larkin studied composition at Brighton Secondary School, then at the Elder Conservatorium of Music at the University of Adelaide.[2][3] Larkin began composing music at a young age and collaborated with filmmaking students while he attended the Elder Conservatorium.[citation needed] In October 2015, he submitted his thesis for an MPhil in music composition, entitled "Orchestrating the wasteland: sample library engineering as an integral process of realising the music for Wastelander Panda".[1]
